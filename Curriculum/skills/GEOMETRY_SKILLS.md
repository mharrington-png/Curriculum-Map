# Geometry Supporting Skills

This is the initial reusable skill library extracted from Math 22. Skills represent smaller techniques and reasoning moves that support multiple geometry objectives.

## Diagram and Notation Skills

- **SK-GEO-FOUNDATIONAL-DEFINITIONS:** Recall definitions of points, lines, planes, segments, rays, angles, and intersections.
- **SK-GEO-NOTATION:** Read and write geometric notation correctly.
- **SK-GEO-DIAGRAM-MARK:** Add congruence, parallel, perpendicular, and angle markings to a diagram.
- **SK-GEO-DIAGRAM-INFER:** Distinguish stated or marked facts from visual appearance.
- **SK-GEO-CORRESPONDENCE:** Match corresponding vertices, sides, and angles in order.
- **SK-GEO-UNITS:** Select and report appropriate linear, square, or angular units.

## Measurement and Algebra Skills

- **SK-GEO-SEGMENT-ADDITION:** Translate segment addition into an equation.
- **SK-GEO-ANGLE-ADDITION:** Translate angle addition into an equation.
- **SK-GEO-BISECTOR:** Use a bisector to create equal measures.
- **SK-GEO-MIDPOINT:** Use a midpoint to create equal segment lengths.
- **SK-GEO-PERIMETER:** Add boundary lengths without omitting or duplicating sides.
- **SK-GEO-AREA-DECOMPOSE:** Decompose a composite region into familiar shapes.
- **SK-GEO-EXACT-APPROX:** Distinguish exact values from measured or rounded approximations.
- **SK-GEO-ANGLE-MEASURE:** Measure or calculate an angle accurately.

## Reasoning and Proof Skills

- **SK-PROOF-GIVEN:** Translate givens into diagram markings and statements.
- **SK-PROOF-DEFINITION:** Apply a geometric definition as justification.
- **SK-PROOF-THEOREM-SELECT:** Select a theorem that connects known facts to a target statement.
- **SK-PROOF-CONGRUENCE-ORDER:** Write congruence statements in corresponding order.
- **SK-PROOF-SIMILARITY-ORDER:** Write similarity statements in corresponding order.
- **SK-PROOF-CPCTC:** Use corresponding parts only after triangle congruence is established.
- **SK-PROOF-CONVERSE:** Recognize and apply the converse of a conditional statement.
- **SK-PROOF-COUNTEREXAMPLE:** Disprove a universal statement with a counterexample.
- **SK-PROOF-STRUCTURE:** Organize statements and reasons into a valid proof sequence.

## Lines and Angles Skills

- **SK-ANG-PAIR-IDENTIFY:** Identify complementary, supplementary, vertical, and linear-pair relationships.
- **SK-ANG-TRANSVERSAL-IDENTIFY:** Identify corresponding, alternate, and same-side angle pairs.
- **SK-ANG-PARALLEL-APPLY:** Use parallel-line angle relationships.
- **SK-ANG-PARALLEL-CONVERSE:** Use angle relationships to prove lines parallel.
- **SK-LIN-PERPENDICULAR-ANGLE:** Use right-angle relationships created by perpendicular lines.

## Triangle Skills

- **SK-TRI-CONGRUENCE-VOCAB:** Recall and correctly use terminology and notation for triangle classification, congruence, and corresponding parts.
- **SK-TRI-RIGHT-VOCAB:** Recall and correctly use the terms leg, hypotenuse, opposite side, adjacent side, and trigonometric ratio.
- **SK-TRI-CLASSIFY:** Classify a triangle by side lengths and angle measures.
- **SK-TRI-ANGLE-SUM:** Apply the triangle angle-sum theorem.
- **SK-TRI-EXTERIOR-ANGLE:** Apply the triangle exterior-angle theorem.
- **SK-TRI-CONGRUENCE-SELECT:** Select SAS, SSS, HL, ASA, or AAS from marked information.
- **SK-TRI-ISOSCELES:** Apply isosceles-triangle theorems and converses.
- **SK-TRI-PYTHAGOREAN:** Apply the Pythagorean theorem or its converse.
- **SK-TRI-SPECIAL-RATIOS:** Recall special-right-triangle ratios.
- **SK-TRI-TRIG-RATIO:** Match opposite, adjacent, and hypotenuse sides to a trigonometric ratio.
- **SK-TRI-GEOMETRIC-MEAN:** Apply geometric-mean relationships in a right triangle split by an altitude.
- **SK-TRI-TRIG-SOLVE-SIDE:** Use a trigonometric ratio to determine a missing side.
- **SK-TRI-TRIG-SOLVE-ANGLE:** Use an inverse trigonometric function to determine a missing angle.

## Polygon and Quadrilateral Skills

- **SK-QUAD-DEFINITIONS:** Recall the definitions and defining properties of the major quadrilateral families.
- **SK-POLY-ANGLE-SUM:** Calculate polygon interior or exterior angle sums.
- **SK-QUAD-HIERARCHY:** Use the inclusive hierarchy of quadrilateral classifications.
- **SK-QUAD-PARALLELOGRAM:** Apply parallelogram properties and tests.
- **SK-QUAD-RECT-RHOM-SQUARE:** Distinguish and apply rectangle, rhombus, and square properties.
- **SK-QUAD-TRAPEZOID:** Apply trapezoid and midsegment relationships.
- **SK-QUAD-KITE:** Apply kite properties.

## Similarity Skills

- **SK-SIM-VOCAB:** Recall and correctly use the terms similarity, dilation, scale factor, and corresponding parts.
- **SK-SIM-SCALE-FACTOR:** Calculate and interpret a similarity or dilation scale factor.
- **SK-SIM-PROPORTION:** Set up proportions using corresponding sides.
- **SK-SIM-AA:** Recognize AA triangle similarity.
- **SK-SIM-SSS-SAS:** Test SSS or SAS triangle similarity.
- **SK-SIM-PERIMETER-AREA:** Relate length scale factor to perimeter and area factors.
- **SK-SIM-TRI-PROPORTIONALITY:** Apply triangle proportionality relationships.

## Circle Skills

- **SK-CIR-VOCAB:** Identify circle parts and relationships from a diagram.
- **SK-CIR-CIRCUMFERENCE:** Calculate circumference from radius or diameter.
- **SK-CIR-ARC-LENGTH:** Calculate arc length from radius and central angle.
- **SK-CIR-AREA:** Calculate the area of a circle.
- **SK-CIR-SECTOR-AREA:** Calculate sector area from radius and central angle.
- **SK-CIR-SEGMENT-AREA:** Calculate segment area by subtracting a triangle from a sector.
- **SK-CIR-ARC-FRACTION:** Relate a central angle to a fraction of a circle.
- **SK-CIR-INSCRIBED-ANGLE:** Relate an inscribed angle to its intercepted arc.
- **SK-CIR-CHORD-RELATION:** Apply equal-chord, equal-arc, and center-distance relationships.
- **SK-CIR-TANGENT-RADIUS:** Use the perpendicular relationship between a radius and tangent.
- **SK-CIR-ANGLE-RELATION:** Select the correct angle formula for chords, secants, or tangents.
- **SK-CIR-SEGMENT-PRODUCT:** Select and apply an intersecting-segment product relationship.
