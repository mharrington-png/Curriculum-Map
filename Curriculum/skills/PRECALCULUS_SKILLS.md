# Precalculus Supporting Skills

This is the initial reusable skill library extracted from Math 49. It supplements, rather than duplicates, algebra, function, and trigonometry skills already listed in `TRIGONOMETRY_SKILLS.md`.

## Function Analysis Skills

- **SK-FUNC-OPERATIONS:** Add, subtract, multiply, and divide function outputs.
- **SK-FUNC-DOMAIN-FORMULA:** Determine domain restrictions from denominators, even roots, and logarithms.
- **SK-FUNC-DOMAIN-COMBINE:** Determine the domain of a sum, difference, product, quotient, or composition.
- **SK-FUNC-SYMMETRY:** Test a formula or graph for even or odd symmetry.
- **SK-FUNC-DECOMPOSE:** Identify simpler inner and outer functions in a composition.
- **SK-FUNC-ONE-TO-ONE:** Test whether a function is one-to-one on a domain.
- **SK-FUNC-INVERSE-VERIFY:** Verify inverse functions using composition.
- **SK-VAR-DIRECT:** Represent and interpret a direct-variation relationship.
- **SK-VAR-INVERSE:** Represent and interpret an inverse-variation relationship.
- **SK-VAR-POWER:** Represent and interpret a power-variation relationship.
- **SK-MODEL-FAMILY-SELECT:** Select a function family whose behavior fits a table, graph, or context.

Math 49 reuses `SK-FUNC-COMPOSE-FORMULA`, `SK-FUNC-COMPOSE-TABLE`, and `SK-FUNC-INVERSE-ALGEBRA` from the advanced algebra library.

## Polynomial and Rational Skills

- **SK-POLY-END-BEHAVIOR:** Infer polynomial end behavior from degree and leading coefficient.
- **SK-POLY-ZEROS:** Determine real and complex zeros using an appropriate method.
- **SK-POLY-MULTIPLICITY:** Relate zero multiplicity to local graph behavior.
- **SK-POLY-CONSTRUCT:** Build a polynomial from zeros, multiplicities, and a point.
- **SK-RAT-HOLE:** Identify a removable discontinuity and its coordinates.
- **SK-RAT-VERTICAL-ASYMPTOTE:** Determine vertical asymptotes from noncanceling denominator zeros.
- **SK-RAT-END-BEHAVIOR:** Determine horizontal or slant asymptotic behavior.
- **SK-RAT-SIGN-INTERVAL:** Determine the sign of a rational function on intervals.

Factoring and cancellation reuse `SK-RAT-RESTRICTIONS`, `SK-RAT-FACTOR`, and `SK-RAT-CANCEL-FACTORS` from the intermediate algebra library.

## Exponential and Logarithmic Skills

- **SK-EXP-REWRITE-BASE:** Rewrite exponential expressions using a common base when possible.
- **SK-LOG-CONVERT:** Convert between logarithmic and exponential forms.

Math 49 reuses the specific logarithm properties, equation-solving, domain-checking, and asymptote skills defined in the advanced algebra library.

## Limit Skills

- **SK-LIM-NOTATION:** Read and write limit notation with correct approach direction.
- **SK-LIM-GRAPH:** Estimate one-sided and two-sided limits from a graph.
- **SK-LIM-TABLE:** Estimate a limit from input-output values approaching from both sides.
- **SK-LIM-SUBSTITUTE:** Test a limit using direct substitution.
- **SK-LIM-SIMPLIFY:** Resolve an indeterminate algebraic form by factoring or rationalizing.
- **SK-LIM-INFINITY:** Compare dominant terms to determine behavior at infinity.
- **SK-LIM-INFINITE:** Interpret unbounded output near a finite input as an infinite limit.
- **SK-LIM-CONTINUITY:** Check the three conditions for continuity at a point.
- **SK-LIM-GROWTH-ORDER:** Apply the long-run growth hierarchy among logarithmic, polynomial, and exponential functions.

## Sequence and Series Skills

- **SK-SEQ-NOTATION:** Interpret subscripts and evaluate terms of a sequence.
- **SK-SEQ-DIFFERENCE:** Recognize constant difference in an arithmetic sequence.
- **SK-SEQ-RATIO:** Recognize constant ratio in a geometric sequence.
- **SK-SEQ-EXPLICIT:** Generate an explicit formula from a sequence pattern.
- **SK-SEQ-RECURSIVE:** Generate a recursive formula with an initial condition.
- **SK-SEQ-GRAPH:** Graph a sequence as discrete ordered pairs.
- **SK-SEQ-MODEL:** Identify initial value and pattern of change in a discrete context.
- **SK-SER-SIGMA:** Expand and evaluate a finite sum written in sigma notation.
- **SK-SER-ARITHMETIC:** Apply an arithmetic-series sum formula.
- **SK-SER-GEOMETRIC-FINITE:** Apply a finite geometric-series sum formula.
- **SK-SER-GEOMETRIC-INFINITE:** Test convergence and sum an infinite geometric series.
- **SK-SER-MODEL:** Identify the initial term, change factor, and number of terms in an application.

## Conic Section Skills

- **SK-CONIC-CLASSIFY:** Classify a circle, ellipse, hyperbola, or parabola from an equation or geometric definition.
- **SK-CONIC-GENERAL-STANDARD:** Complete squares to convert a general conic equation to standard form.
- **SK-CONIC-CIRCLE-EQUATIONS:** Connect implicit, standard, and parametric equations of a circle.
- **SK-CONIC-ELLIPSE-EQUATIONS:** Connect implicit, standard, and parametric equations of an ellipse.
- **SK-CONIC-HYPERBOLA-EQUATIONS:** Connect implicit, standard, and parametric equations of a hyperbola.
- **SK-CONIC-CIRCLE-FEATURES:** Determine and use a circle's center and radius.
- **SK-CONIC-ELLIPSE-FEATURES:** Determine and use an ellipse's center, axes, vertices, and foci.
- **SK-CONIC-HYPERBOLA-FEATURES:** Determine and use a hyperbola's center, axes, vertices, foci, and asymptotes.
- **SK-CONIC-DISTANCE-DEFINITION:** Use fixed-distance and focal-distance relationships to explain circles, ellipses, and hyperbolas.
- **SK-CONIC-PARABOLA-DEFINITION:** Explain a parabola using its focus-directrix distance relationship.
