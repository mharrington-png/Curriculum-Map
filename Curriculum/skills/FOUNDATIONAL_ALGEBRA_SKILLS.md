# Foundational Algebra Supporting Skills

This is the initial reusable skill library extracted from Math 12. It is intended to support Math 12 and recur throughout later mathematics courses.

## Numerical and Expression Skills

- **SK-NUM-PERCENT-CALCULATE:** Calculate a percentage of a quantity or determine what percent one quantity is of another.
- **SK-NUM-PERCENT-CHANGE:** Calculate and interpret percent increase or decrease.
- **SK-NUM-ORDER-OPERATIONS:** Apply the order of operations to a numerical expression.
- **SK-NUM-INTEGER-OPERATE:** Add, subtract, multiply, and divide signed integers.
- **SK-NUM-FRACTION-OPERATE:** Add, subtract, multiply, and divide fractions.
- **SK-NUM-PRIME-FACTOR:** Express a whole number as a product of prime factors.
- **SK-ALG-LIKE-TERMS:** Identify and combine like terms.
- **SK-ALG-DISTRIBUTE:** Apply the distributive property in either direction.
- **SK-ALG-EXPRESSION-SIMPLIFY:** Simplify an algebraic expression using operation properties.

## Equation and Inequality Skills

- **SK-ALG-INVERSE-OPERATIONS:** Use inverse operations to maintain an equivalent equation.
- **SK-ALG-CLEAR-FRACTIONS:** Clear fractions from an equation using a common denominator.
- **SK-ALG-CROSS-MULTIPLY:** Use cross multiplication when two ratios form a proportion.
- **SK-ALG-VARIABLE-ISOLATE:** Isolate a specified variable in an equation or formula.
- **SK-ALG-TRANSLATE-EQUATION:** Translate quantities and relationships into an equation.
- **SK-ALG-CHECK-SOLUTION:** Check a proposed solution in the original equation.
- **SK-INE-REVERSE-SIGN:** Reverse an inequality when multiplying or dividing by a negative value.
- **SK-INE-NUMBER-LINE:** Represent an inequality solution on a number line.
- **SK-INE-COMPOUND-AND:** Interpret AND as the intersection of two inequality solution sets.
- **SK-INE-COMPOUND-OR:** Interpret OR as the union of two inequality solution sets.
- **SK-NOT-INTERVAL:** Translate between inequality and interval notation.
- **SK-ABS-SPLIT-EQUATION:** Rewrite an absolute-value equation as two cases.
- **SK-ABS-SPLIT-INEQUALITY:** Rewrite an absolute-value inequality as a compound inequality.
- **SK-INE-GRAPH-BOUNDARY:** Graph the boundary line of a linear inequality with the correct line type.
- **SK-INE-SYSTEM-REGION:** Identify the overlapping solution region of multiple linear inequalities.
- **SK-NOT-INEQUALITY-READ:** Read inequality symbols and state the relationship each symbol expresses.

## Line and Function Skills

- **SK-COORD-DISTANCE-AXIS:** Calculate horizontal or vertical distance from the difference of coordinates.
- **SK-COORD-DISTANCE-PYTHAGOREAN:** Calculate non-axis-aligned distance using the Pythagorean theorem or distance formula.
- **SK-COORD-MIDPOINT:** Calculate the midpoint of a segment from endpoint coordinates.
- **SK-LIN-SLOPE-POINTS:** Calculate slope from two points.
- **SK-LIN-SLOPE-GRAPH:** Determine slope from a graph.
- **SK-LIN-PLOT-INTERCEPT:** Plot an intercept accurately on a coordinate plane.
- **SK-LIN-GRAPH-SLOPE:** Use slope to generate additional points on a line.
- **SK-LIN-FORM-CONVERT:** Convert among common forms of a linear equation.
- **SK-LIN-PARALLEL-SLOPE:** Recognize that distinct parallel lines have equal slopes.
- **SK-LIN-PERPENDICULAR-SLOPE:** Determine the negative reciprocal of a nonzero slope.
- **SK-FUNC-NOTATION-READ:** Identify the input and output represented by function notation.
- **SK-FUNC-VOCABULARY:** Recall and correctly use the terms relation, function, input, output, domain, and range.
- **SK-FUNC-VERTICAL-LINE:** Apply the vertical line test to a graph.
- **SK-FUNC-DISCRETE-CONTINUOUS:** Distinguish discrete inputs from values varying continuously over an interval.
- **SK-FUNC-DOMAIN-RANGE-GRAPH:** Read domain and range from a graph.
- **SK-FUNC-DOMAIN-RANGE-TABLE:** Read domain and range from a table.

## System Skills

- **SK-SYS-GRAPH-INTERSECTION:** Locate and interpret the intersection of two lines.
- **SK-SYS-SUBSTITUTE:** Substitute an equivalent expression for a variable.
- **SK-SYS-ELIMINATE:** Add equations to eliminate a variable.
- **SK-SYS-MODEL:** Define two variables and write two equations for a context.
- **SK-SYS-CHECK:** Check an ordered pair in both equations of a system.

## Polynomial Skills

- **SK-POLY-VOCABULARY:** Identify terms, factors, coefficients, variables, and degree in a polynomial expression.
- **SK-EXP-PRODUCT:** Apply the product rule for exponents.
- **SK-EXP-QUOTIENT:** Apply the quotient rule for exponents.
- **SK-EXP-POWER:** Apply the power rule for exponents.
- **SK-POLY-LIKE-TERMS:** Identify like polynomial terms by variable part and exponent.
- **SK-POLY-DISTRIBUTE:** Multiply a monomial by a polynomial.
- **SK-POLY-BINOMIAL-MULTIPLY:** Multiply two binomials using distribution.
- **SK-POLY-DIVIDE-MONOMIAL:** Divide each term of a polynomial by a monomial.
- **SK-POLY-GCF:** Determine the numerical and variable greatest common factor of polynomial terms.
