# Intermediate Algebra Supporting Skills

This is the initial reusable skill library extracted from Math 21. Foundational skills already listed in `FOUNDATIONAL_ALGEBRA_SKILLS.md` should be reused rather than duplicated.

## Function Skills

- **SK-FUNC-INPUT-OUTPUT:** Identify input and output values in a formula, graph, table, or context.
- **SK-FUNC-SOLVE-INPUT:** Solve for an input associated with a specified output.
- **SK-FUNC-AROC:** Calculate average rate of change over an interval.
- **SK-FUNC-AROC-INTERPRET:** Interpret average rate of change with contextual units.
- **SK-FUNC-COMPARE-REP:** Compare functions shown in different representations.
- **SK-FUNC-CONTEXT-DOMAIN:** Restrict domain to values meaningful in a context.
- **SK-FUNC-CONTEXT-RANGE:** Interpret the range of a function in a context.

## Factoring Skills

- **SK-FACTOR-GCF:** Factor the greatest common monomial factor from a polynomial.
- **SK-FACTOR-TRINOMIAL-MONIC:** Factor a monic quadratic trinomial.
- **SK-FACTOR-TRINOMIAL-GENERAL:** Factor a quadratic trinomial with a nonunit leading coefficient.
- **SK-FACTOR-DIFF-SQUARES:** Factor a difference of two squares.
- **SK-FACTOR-ZERO-PRODUCT:** Apply the zero-product property.
- **SK-FACTOR-CHECK:** Verify a factorization by multiplication.

## Rational Expression Skills

- **SK-RAT-RESTRICTIONS:** Determine excluded values from an original denominator.
- **SK-RAT-FACTOR:** Factor numerators and denominators completely.
- **SK-RAT-CANCEL-FACTORS:** Cancel common factors rather than individual terms.
- **SK-RAT-LCD:** Determine the least common denominator of rational expressions.
- **SK-RAT-COMBINE:** Combine rational expressions over a common denominator.
- **SK-RAT-CLEAR-DENOMINATORS:** Clear denominators from a rational equation.
- **SK-RAT-EXTRANEOUS:** Check candidate solutions against denominator restrictions.

## Exponent and Radical Skills

- **SK-EXP-NEGATIVE:** Rewrite a negative exponent using a reciprocal.
- **SK-EXP-ZERO:** Apply the zero-exponent rule.
- **SK-EXP-RATIONAL:** Interpret numerator and denominator roles in a rational exponent.
- **SK-RAD-FACTOR-PERFECT:** Extract perfect-power factors from a radical.
- **SK-RAD-CONVERT:** Convert between radical and rational-exponent notation.
- **SK-RAD-ISOLATE:** Isolate a radical expression before solving.
- **SK-RAD-CHECK:** Check a candidate solution for extraneous results.

## Quadratic Skills

- **SK-QUAD-FORM-IDENTIFY:** Identify standard, factored, and vertex forms.
- **SK-QUAD-FORM-CONVERT:** Rewrite a quadratic among standard, factored, and vertex forms using an appropriate algebraic method.
- **SK-QUAD-VERTEX:** Determine the vertex and axis of symmetry.
- **SK-QUAD-INTERCEPTS:** Determine x- and y-intercepts of a quadratic function.
- **SK-QUAD-SQUARE-ROOT:** Apply the square-root property with both signs.
- **SK-QUAD-COMPLETE-SQUARE:** Create a perfect-square trinomial by completing the square.
- **SK-QUAD-FORMULA:** Substitute coefficients accurately into the quadratic formula.
- **SK-QUAD-DISCRIMINANT:** Use the discriminant to predict the number and type of solutions.
- **SK-QUAD-METHOD-SELECT:** Select an efficient quadratic-solving method from equation structure.
- **SK-QUAD-MODEL-FEATURES:** Interpret vertex and zeros in an application.

## Complex Number Skills

- **SK-CPLX-I-POWERS:** Simplify integer powers of the imaginary unit.
- **SK-CPLX-OPERATE:** Add, subtract, and multiply complex numbers.
- **SK-CPLX-QUAD-SOLUTION:** Express a quadratic solution with a negative discriminant in complex form.
