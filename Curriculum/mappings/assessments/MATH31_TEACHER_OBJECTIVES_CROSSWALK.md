# Math 31 Teacher Objectives Reconciliation

This record documents how the five teacher-curated objective files were incorporated into the canonical Math 31 curriculum map.

## Source-to-Curriculum Crosswalk

| Teacher source | Canonical curriculum unit | Canonical objective families |
|---|---|---|
| `Copy of Unit_1_LO.docx` | Required Unit 1: Linear Functions and Function Notation | `M31-LIN-001` through `M31-LIN-012` |
| `Copy of Unit_2_LO.docx` | Required Unit 2: Function Behavior and Structure | `M31-FUN-001` through `M31-FUN-018` |
| `Copy of Unit_3_LO.docx` | Required Unit 3: Quadratic Functions and Equations | `M31-QUAD-001` through `M31-QUAD-007` |
| `Copy of Unit_4_LO.docx` | Required Unit 4: Exponential Functions and Models | `M31-EXP-001` through `M31-EXP-010` |
| `Copy of Unit_5_LO.docx` | Required Unit 5: Logarithmic Functions and Models | `M31-LOG-001` through `M31-LOG-012` |

## Material Changes to the Earlier Draft

- Linear functions moved from general Review into a required review-and-deepen Unit 1.
- Function notation, independent/dependent variables, interval behavior, total change, and rate units were added explicitly.
- Inverse-function units and composition meaning/units were added.
- Horizontal-line testing and graphing inverse functions are required Math 31 outcomes, overriding their optional label in the teacher source.
- Quadratics remain required and now occupy Unit 3, matching the teacher sequence.
- Exponential modeling from two points, growth-rate comparison, and continuous percent change were added.
- Nominal/effective rate language was removed as a standalone objective because it does not appear in the curated objectives; compounding remains required.
- Advanced logarithmic equations, intersections of exponentials, equivalent discrete/continuous forms, and modeling were added.

## Review Content Embedded in Units

The teacher files identify some prerequisite review inside required units. The canonical map records these globally as Review content while retaining them as entry skills for their relevant units:

- Polynomial vocabulary, binomial multiplication, and simple quadratic solving before Unit 3
- Exponent properties and percentage fluency before Unit 4
- Average rate of change revisited before concavity in Unit 2

## Items Requiring Faculty Judgment

- Whether increasing/decreasing intervals belong primarily in Unit 1 or Unit 2
- The assessment depth expected for concavity from tables
- Whether solving exponential equations during exponent review is prerequisite review or should occur only in Unit 5
- Whether any nominal/effective rate distinction should be restored as a required objective

## Scope Note

Textbook page and exercise references inside the teacher files remain source-level resource notes. They were not promoted into the OpenStax map because they appear to reference a different textbook or workbook. They can be mapped separately once the resource title is identified.
