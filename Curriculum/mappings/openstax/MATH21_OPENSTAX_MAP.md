# Math 21 OpenStax Mapping

This draft translates the existing Math 21 course map into an objective-centered resource crosswalk. OpenStax sections are replaceable resources; the Math 21 objectives remain authoritative.

## Resource Record and Alignment Labels

- **Resource:** OpenStax *Intermediate Algebra 2e* (Marecek and Mathis, 2020)
- **Mapping level:** Textbook section to learning objective; examples and exercises are not yet mapped
- **Direct:** Explicitly teaches the LO
- **Partial:** Teaches only part of the LO or needs careful selection
- **Supporting:** Supplies prerequisite or adjacent content
- **Gap:** No direct match in the department's currently approved section range

## Alignment and Resource Gaps

1. Fall and Spring sections should reach the same endpoint and cover the same required objectives.
2. Functions need graph, table, formula, and contextual resources beyond the textbook.
3. Use sections 4.1-4.2 selectively after a short common systems diagnostic rather than teaching a full review unit.
4. Textbook questions must be screened for premature dependence on complex numbers.
5. Assessment items should be tagged to objective IDs.

The section titles and scope were checked against OpenStax *Intermediate Algebra 2e*. Faculty should confirm this edition and decide whether 6.5, 8.6, and 8.8 should be added to the approved range.
