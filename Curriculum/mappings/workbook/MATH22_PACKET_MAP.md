# Math 22 Geometry Packet Mapping

This mapping attaches the Fall 2025 geometry packet to curriculum objectives. The packet is a replaceable resource; objectives and skills remain authoritative.

All six packet units are required. Math 22 has no designated extension material, and formal constructions are outside the course scope.
