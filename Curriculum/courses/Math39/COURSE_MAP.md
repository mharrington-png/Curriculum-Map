# Middlesex Math Course Overview

## MATH 39

**Course title:** Precalculus with Data Analysis  
**Status:** Approved curriculum structure and objective-to-skill mapping.

Math 39 follows Math 32 and provides an optional route before Math 49. Because students do not all take Math 39, a skill may appropriately be introduced in both Math 39 and Math 49.

## Required Content

- Linear modeling, correlation, residuals, and model assessment
- Exponential modeling, regression, half-life, and doubling time
- Power functions, proportionality, regression, and long-run dominance
- Function combinations and polynomial behavior
- Describing categorical and quantitative data
- Measures of center and variation, boxplots, percentiles, and z-scores
- Empirical, conditional, theoretical, and compound probability

## Extension Material

- Expected value and its use in long-run decisions
- Linearization of exponential and power data
